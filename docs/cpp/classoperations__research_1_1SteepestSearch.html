Detailed Description

Once we have a first solution to the problem, there may be (most often, there are) elements in S that are covered several times. To decrease the total cost, SteepestSearch tries to eliminate some redundant T_j's from the solution or equivalently, to flip some x_j's from 1 to 0. the algorithm gets its name because it goes in the steepest immediate direction, taking the T_j with the largest total cost.
