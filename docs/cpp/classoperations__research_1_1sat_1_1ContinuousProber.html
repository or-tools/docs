SatSolver::Status operations_research::sat::ContinuousProber::Probe ( )

Starts or continues probing variables and their bounds. It returns:

  • SatSolver::INFEASIBLE if the problem is proven infeasible.
  • SatSolver::FEASIBLE when a feasible solution is found
  • SatSolver::LIMIT_REACHED if the limit stored in the model is reached Calling Probe() after it has returned FEASIBLE or LIMIT_REACHED will resume probing from its previous state.

Continuous probing procedure.
