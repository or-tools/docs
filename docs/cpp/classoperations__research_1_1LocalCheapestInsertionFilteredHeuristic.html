Detailed Description

Filter-base decision builder which builds a solution by inserting nodes at their cheapest position. The cost of a position is computed an arc-based cost callback. Node selected for insertion are considered in decreasing order of distance to the start/ends of the routes, i.e. farthest nodes are inserted first.
