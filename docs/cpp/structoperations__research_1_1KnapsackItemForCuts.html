Detailed Description

--— KnapsackItemForCuts --— KnapsackItemForCuts is a small struct to pair an item weight with its corresponding profit. The aim of the knapsack problem is to pack as many valuable items as possible. A straight forward heuristic is to take those with the greatest profit-per-unit-weight. This ratio is called efficiency in this implementation. So items will be grouped in vectors, and sorted by decreasing efficiency.
